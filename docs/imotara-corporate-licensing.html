What is Imotara?
Imotara is an AI-powered emotional wellness companion — a private, judgment-free space where people can process feelings, build daily emotional awareness, and get support in their own language and tone. It combines mood check-ins, a personalised companion persona, voice & text conversation, and gentle long-term memory — available on web, iOS and Android, with an offline mode that keeps core support working even without internet.

Data Privacy, Security & Onboarding
Confidential · July 2026
Built so HR can report on wellbeing without seeing it.
Corporate accounts are designed around one rule: organisations get proof the programme works, employees keep their privacy completely intact.
Trust & data handling
🙈
No individual data, ever
Org admins only ever see aggregate metrics — weekly active users, average session length, check-in rate. No conversation, mood entry, or identity is exposed at the organisation level.
🛡️
Row-level access control
Database-level access rules ensure org admins and members can only reach data scoped to their own organisation — enforced server-side, not just in the app.
🔐
Encrypted in transit
All traffic between the app, dashboard and servers is encrypted (TLS). Payment details are handled by certified payment processors — Imotara never stores card data.
📋
Full audit trail
Every membership, invite and role change in your organisation is logged with timestamp and actor, viewable and exportable from the dashboard at any time.
👤
Personal plans stay personal
If an employee already has a personal Imotara subscription, it's never downgraded by the org licence — the higher tier always wins.
🌐
Privacy-by-design programme
Data handling practices are being formalised against GDPR and India's DPDP Act as part of an ongoing compliance programme; details available on request for enterprise agreements.

Frequently asked questions
Q What features do employees actually get?
Every covered employee receives the full individual Pro-tier experience — unlimited AI conversations, unlimited history, neural voice, mood timeline, companion letters, and cross-device sync. They see an org badge in Settings, but the app looks and works the same as a personal subscription.
Q Can our HR team see what employees discuss?
No. HR only ever sees anonymised, aggregate data — trends across the whole organisation, never a single employee's identity, mood entry, or conversation. This is enforced at the database level, not just hidden in the interface.
Q How fast can we roll this out?
Once your quote is confirmed, your organisation and seats are typically provisioned the same day. Employees are invited via a single email or CSV upload and get access as soon as they accept.
Q What if an employee leaves the company?
Removing a member from the dashboard immediately revokes their org-provided access and frees up the seat for reassignment. If they had a personal subscription beforehand, that continues unaffected.
Q Do you support non-English speaking teams?
Yes — 22 languages including Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi and more, with gendered grammar handling and voice support, making Imotara genuinely usable across frontline and multi-lingual workforces.
Q Can we integrate this with our own HR systems?
Yes — Corporate accounts can generate scoped API keys to pull aggregate usage stats and member lists into internal HR or analytics tools.
