What is Imotara?
Imotara is an AI-powered emotional wellness companion — a private, judgment-free space where students can process feelings, build daily emotional awareness, and get support in their own language and tone. Mood check-ins, a personalised companion persona, voice & text conversation, and gentle long-term memory — available on web, iOS and Android, with an offline mode that keeps core support working even without reliable internet.
Why institutions choose Imotara
🌙
Support beyond office hours
Counselling centres close. Emotional distress doesn't follow office hours. Imotara gives students somewhere to turn at 2am before a crisis, not just after one.
🗣️
Meets students in their language
Support in Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu and more — critical for first-generation and multi-lingual students who may not feel comfortable in English-only services.
🔒
Privacy that builds trust
Students are far more likely to open up when they know no counsellor, teacher, or administrator can see their conversation. Imotara is built around that guarantee.

Student Privacy, Safety & Onboarding
Confidential · July 2026
Built so your institution can measure impact without seeing a single student's data.
Educational accounts are designed around one rule: your wellbeing office gets proof the programme is working, students keep their privacy completely intact.
Trust & data handling
🙈
No individual data, ever
Wellbeing staff only ever see aggregate metrics — weekly active students, average session length, check-in rate. No conversation, mood entry, or identity is exposed at the institution level.
🛡️
Row-level access control
Database-level access rules ensure admins and students can only reach data scoped to their own institution — enforced server-side, not just in the app.
🔐
Encrypted in transit
All traffic between the app, dashboard and servers is encrypted (TLS). Imotara never stores card or payment data.
📋
Full audit trail
Every membership, invite and role change in your institution is logged with timestamp and actor, viewable and exportable from the dashboard at any time.
🎂
Eligibility is your institution's call
Your institution controls enrolment (e.g. by verified student email domain) and is responsible for age-appropriate consent under your own policies — Imotara does not independently verify a student's age.
🌐
Privacy-by-design programme
Data handling practices are being formalised against GDPR and India's DPDP Act as part of an ongoing compliance programme; details available on request for institutional agreements.

Frequently asked questions
Q What features do students actually get?
Every covered student receives the full individual Plus-tier experience — unlimited AI conversations, 90-day history, neural voice, mood timeline, companion letters, and cross-device sync.
Q Can our staff see what students discuss?
No. Staff only ever see anonymised, aggregate data — trends across the whole institution, never a single student's identity, mood entry, or conversation.
Q How do we verify who counts as a student?
Most institutions invite students via a verified institutional email domain or CSV roster. Your institution controls eligibility criteria.
Q What happens when a student graduates or leaves?
Removing a member from the dashboard immediately revokes their institution-provided access and frees the seat for reassignment.
Q Do you support non-English speaking students?
Yes — 22 languages including Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi and more, with gendered grammar handling and voice support.
Q Is this a replacement for our counselling centre?
No — Imotara is designed to complement, not replace, professional counselling and crisis services. It's a private, always-available layer of everyday emotional support.
