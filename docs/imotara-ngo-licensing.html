What is Imotara?
Imotara is an AI-powered emotional wellness companion — a private, judgment-free space for people to process feelings, build daily emotional awareness, and get support in their own language and tone. Mood check-ins, a personalised companion persona, voice & text conversation, and gentle long-term memory — available on web, iOS and Android, with an offline mode that keeps core support working even without a reliable internet connection.
Why NGOs choose Imotara
🌍
Reaches who outreach can't
Field workers can't be everywhere. Imotara is a private app on a beneficiary's own phone — available at 2am, in their own language, with no travel or waiting.
🔒
Privacy for vulnerable groups
For survivors of violence, at-risk youth, or marginalised communities, privacy isn't a nice-to-have — it's the reason they'll actually use it. No individual data is ever exposed to your organisation.
📊

Data Privacy, Security & Onboarding
Confidential · July 2026
Built so you can report impact without ever seeing it.
NGO accounts are designed around one rule: your organisation gets proof the programme works, every beneficiary keeps their privacy completely intact — which matters most for the people you serve.
Trust & data handling
🙈
No individual data, ever
Org admins only ever see aggregate metrics — weekly active users, average session length, check-in rate. No conversation, mood entry, or identity is exposed at the organisation level.
🛡️
Row-level access control
Database-level access rules ensure org admins and beneficiaries can only reach data scoped to their own organisation — enforced server-side, not just in the app.
🔐
Encrypted in transit
All traffic between the app, dashboard and servers is encrypted (TLS). Imotara never stores card or payment data.
📋
Full audit trail
Every membership, invite and role change in your organisation is logged with timestamp and actor, viewable and exportable from the dashboard at any time.
👤
Personal plans stay personal
If a beneficiary already has a personal Imotara subscription, it's never downgraded by your org licence — the higher tier always wins.
🌐
Privacy-by-design programme
Data handling practices are being formalised against GDPR and India's DPDP Act as part of an ongoing compliance programme; details available on request for NGO agreements.

Frequently asked questions
Q What proof of registration is needed?
No proof is required for standard self-serve seat packs (10–500) — the 60% NGO rate applies at checkout. For larger custom deployments, or if you'd like registration documentation (e.g. Section 8 / Trust / Society registration) on file for your own grant or donor reporting, our dashboard has an optional verification upload.
Q Can our team see what beneficiaries discuss?
No. Your admin dashboard only ever shows anonymised, aggregate data — never a single beneficiary's identity, mood entry, or conversation.
Q Can we use this for grant reporting?
Yes — aggregate engagement metrics (WAU, session duration, check-in rate) can support your grant and donor reporting today; a dedicated Impact PDF export is on our roadmap.
Q What if a beneficiary already uses Imotara personally?
Their personal licence is never downgraded. If your org's tier is higher, they enjoy those features while their membership with you is active.
Q How fast can we roll this out to the field?
Once your subsidy tier is confirmed, your organisation and seats are typically provisioned the same day. Bulk-invite via CSV gets your whole cohort onboarded quickly.
Q Do you support multiple regional languages?
Yes — 22 languages including Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi and more, with gendered grammar handling and voice support, built for genuinely underserved communities.
