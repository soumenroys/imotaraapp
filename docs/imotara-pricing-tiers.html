When to choose tokens vs subscription
🪙
Choose Tokens if…
You use Imotara occasionally (a few days per week), don't want a recurring charge, or want to try cloud AI beyond the 20/day free limit without committing to a subscription. Tokens give full flexibility — spend only when you choose to.
📅
Choose Plus / Pro if…
You chat with Imotara daily, want companion letters, full history, and cross-device sync without worrying about limits. At 20+ messages per week, Plus at ₹99/mo gives you unlimited replies for the same money that buys just 250 tokens (barely 12 days of heavy use) — the better deal once you're chatting daily.

Organisation FAQ
Q What features do org members get?
All org members receive Pro-tier features (or Plus for EDU). They see an org tier badge in their Settings but otherwise use Imotara identically to a personal subscriber. Individual emotional data is never visible to org admins.
Q Can admins see what employees talk about?
Never. Org admins see only anonymised aggregate data — WAU trends, check-in rates, emotional themes at population level. No individual conversation, message, or identity is exposed to the organisation.
Q What happens if a member already has a personal plan?
Their personal license is never downgraded. If the org tier is lower, the personal plan takes precedence. If the org tier is higher, they enjoy the org's features while membership is active.
Q How do we onboard members?
Via the org admin dashboard at imotara.com/org/dashboard — invite by email or upload a CSV. Members receive an invite link; on acceptance, their Imotara account is automatically provisioned with the org's tier.
