Frequently asked questions
Q Is there a cost to join as a partner?
No — the Referral Partner tier is free to join with no minimum commitment. Certified Reseller and Strategic Partner tiers are by application based on activity level.
Q How is commission calculated and paid?
Commission is a percentage of the net subscription value of each attributed account, reconciled and paid quarterly via bank transfer or UPI, with a per-client statement.
Q What can I resell — just individual plans?
No — partners can refer across all four licensing categories: Individual, Corporate, NGO and Educational, each with its own commission calculation.
Q Is referral tracking automated?
Not yet — referrals are currently tracked via your unique code and reconciled manually by our partnerships team each quarter. A self-serve dashboard is on our roadmap.
Q Can I see what my referred clients discuss on Imotara?
No — partner access never includes visibility into any client's individual conversations or data. Our privacy guarantees apply equally to every account, regardless of how it was referred.
Q Is there a contract or lock-in period?
Referral Partners sign a simple agreement with no lock-in and can stop at any time. Certified Reseller and Strategic Partner terms are discussed individually.
