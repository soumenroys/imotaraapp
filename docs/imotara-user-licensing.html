What is Imotara?
Imotara is an AI-powered emotional wellness companion — a private, judgment-free space to process feelings, build daily emotional awareness, and get support in your own language and tone. Mood check-ins, a personalised companion persona, voice & text conversation, and gentle long-term memory — available on web, iOS and Android, with an offline mode that keeps core support working even without internet.

Your Data, Your Privacy & Getting Started
Confidential · July 2026
Your conversations belong to you — not anyone else.
Imotara is built around one rule: your data is yours to see, export, or delete, whenever you choose.
Trust & data handling
🔐
Encrypted in transit
All traffic between the app and Imotara's servers is encrypted (TLS). Payment details are handled by certified payment processors — Imotara never stores your card data.
📤
Export your data anytime
Pro subscribers can export their full conversation and mood history as JSON or CSV, whenever they want a personal copy.
🗑️
Delete your account anytime
Account deletion is available directly in Settings on both web and mobile — no support ticket required.
📶
Offline AI, on-device
On mobile, offline replies are generated on your device — nothing is sent to a server when you're using offline mode.
🙅
Never sold or shared
Your conversations are never sold to advertisers or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
🌐
Privacy-by-design programme
Data handling practices are being formalised against GDPR and India's DPDP Act as part of an ongoing compliance programme.

Frequently asked questions
Q Is the Free plan really free forever?
Yes — 20 cloud AI messages per day, offline AI, mood check-ins and 7-day history, with no card required and no time limit.
Q What happens when I hit my daily Free limit?
Imotara never hard-blocks you — you're gently switched to offline AI for the rest of the day, or you can use a token top-up for extra cloud messages.
Q Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — monthly Plus and Pro subscriptions can be cancelled anytime from Settings; you keep access until the end of your billing period.
Q Do tokens expire?
No — token top-ups never expire. Use them whenever you like, on any plan.
Q Can I switch between plans?
Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or move between monthly and annual billing anytime from Settings.
Q Is my data private from Imotara's own team?
Your conversations power your own AI replies and are never sold or shared with third parties for marketing. You can export or delete your data anytime.
