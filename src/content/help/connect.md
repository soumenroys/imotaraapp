# Imotara Connect

Real human wellness companions, on your terms: browsing, wallet and minutes, sessions and billing, reviews, refunds — plus how to become a companion yourself.

## What is Imotara Connect?

Connect lets you have a real-time **text chat session with a verified human wellness companion**, billed per minute from prepaid minutes you buy in advance. Companions set their own per-minute rate, and you only ever pay for minutes actually used.

Two important things up front:

- **Connect is 18+ only.** When you first open Connect you'll be asked to confirm your age; users under 18 cannot start sessions. This is enforced, not just a checkbox.
- **Connect companions are not therapists.** These are supportive, non-clinical companions — a friendly listener, a mentor, a calming presence — not licensed medical or mental-health professionals. If you need clinical help, please see our Safety & Crisis article.

Connect is available on every plan; find it at **imotara.com/connect** or the Connect tab in the mobile app.

## Browsing companions

1. Open **Connect**. You'll see approved companions, with online companions and highly-rated companions surfacing first.
2. Each card shows the companion's name, photo, bio, specialty tags, languages, role (e.g. wellness companion, mentor-style, yoga instructor), per-minute rate, online status, and rating.
3. Filter by language, specialty, gender, role, or online-now.
4. Tap a card for the full profile.
5. **Favorites:** tap the heart on a card to save a companion to your favorites (up to 100). If you already have paid minutes with a companion, the card shows your remaining balance with them.

## Paying for sessions: wallet and minutes

There are two simple money concepts in Connect:

1. **Your Imotara wallet** — a general prepaid balance in INR. Top it up in the wallet tab: enter an amount (₹1 up to ₹50,000), accept the Wallet Terms, and pay through the Razorpay checkout. Your balance updates right away.
2. **Minutes with a specific companion** — sessions are started from prepaid minutes purchased **for that companion**. From a companion's profile, choose how many minutes you'd like (1 to 1,000), and pay through checkout. The price is simply their per-minute rate × minutes.

You need at least 1 minute of balance with a companion to start a session with them.

### If a recharge shows as "pending"
Occasionally a payment window closes at the wrong moment. Don't worry:

- If your payment **succeeded**, our payment confirmation completes it automatically — your minutes appear without you doing anything.
- If your payment **didn't go through**, the pending recharge clears itself within about **30 minutes**, after which you can start a fresh one.
- If money left your account but minutes never arrived after that, email **info@imotara.com** with your payment reference and we'll put it right.

## Starting a session

### Instant session
1. Pick a companion who is **online**, make sure you have at least 1 minute of balance with them, and tap **Start session**.
2. The companion is notified and accepts your request — you'll get a notification the moment they do, and the chat begins.
3. If the companion is busy or goes offline, you'll be told and nothing is charged.

### Scheduled session
1. From a companion's profile, choose a scheduled session instead: pick a date and time (up to **90 days** ahead), a duration (from 5 minutes up to 8 hours), and include a short note about what you'd like to talk about.
2. The companion accepts the request, and you'll both be reminded when the time comes.
3. If a scheduled request is never accepted, it expires quietly a couple of hours after the scheduled time — **you are not charged** for a session that never happened.

You can have one open session at a time. Cancel a pending request whenever you like — no charge.

## How per-minute billing works

- The per-minute rate is **locked when your session is created** — if the companion changes their rate later, your session keeps the rate you agreed to.
- Billing counts only **minutes actually used**, one minute at a time, while the session is active.
- When your prepaid minutes with that companion run out, the session ends gracefully on its own — there's no way to be charged beyond what you prepaid.
- If your app crashes or your connection drops, the session closes automatically within about 15 minutes and you're only billed for the minutes that actually elapsed.

### Translation option (+10%)
If you and your companion prefer different languages, you can turn on **live translation** when starting the session. Messages are translated in real time both ways. This adds a **10% surcharge** to the per-minute rate, applied at session creation so there are no surprises.

## Ending a session and leaving a review

1. Either you or the companion can end the session at any time with the end control — you're only billed for minutes used.
2. After a session completes, you can leave a **review**: a 1–5 star rating and an optional short note (up to 200 characters). One review per session, and it feeds the companion's public rating.

## Refunds of unused balance

Your money stays yours:

1. Unused wallet balance can be refunded on request. Submit the request in-app from the wallet section, providing either **bank details** (account number, IFSC, account holder name) or a **UPI ID**.
2. You'll receive a confirmation with a reference number, and the refund is processed to your bank/UPI within about **7 business days**.
3. Minutes already used in sessions aren't refundable — a real person was paid for that time. If you believe you were over-charged for a session, email **info@imotara.com** and we'll review it.

### Wallet inactivity policy
- Your wallet stays active as long as you use it — any top-up or session resets the clock.
- After **2 years** of no activity, the wallet is marked **dormant**. **Your balance is preserved — it is never zeroed.** You'll receive reminder emails well before this happens (at 180, 90, 30, 14, 7, and 1 days), plus an annual statement.
- A dormant balance remains **refundable for 1 year** — just submit a refund request in-app or email **info@imotara.com**.

## Becoming a companion

If you're a warm, steady presence and want to support others, we'd love to hear from you.

### Applying
1. Go to **imotara.com/connect/register** (sign in first).
2. Fill in your profile: display name, a bio (up to 500 characters), your expertise tags and languages, your role style (wellness companion, friend-style, mentor figure, yoga/fitness instructor, and more), and your **per-minute rate** and currency (INR, USD, EUR, GBP, AED, SGD, or AUD).
3. Upload your **verification documents** (ID/credentials) and a profile photo.
4. Read and agree to the **Code of Conduct** — this is required, and it's the heart of what keeps Connect safe and kind.
5. Submit. You'll receive a confirmation email, and Imotara reviews applications within **1–3 business days**. You'll get an email with the decision either way (and a reason, if it's not approved yet).

### Once approved
1. Your profile becomes publicly visible in Connect browse.
2. Set your availability and toggle yourself **online** whenever you're ready to receive session requests.
3. Session requests arrive as push notifications — accept to begin, or decline if it's not a good time.
4. You can keep private session notes for yourself, and block a user if a conversation crosses a line.

### Earnings and payouts
- You earn **80%** of every billed minute; Imotara keeps 20% as a platform fee.
- Track your earnings and completed sessions in your consultant dashboard.
- Request a **payout** whenever your balance reaches the minimum — **₹500** (or $10 for USD earnings) — to your UPI, bank account, or PayPal. Payouts are reviewed and processed by the Imotara team, and you'll be notified when yours is on its way.

### The ground rules
- Connect is **non-clinical**: companions listen, encourage, and support — they never diagnose, prescribe, or present themselves as therapists.
- The **Code of Conduct** applies at all times: kindness, honesty, privacy, and clear boundaries. Every session includes an **emergency help** button that routes users to crisis resources; if a user seems to be in danger, guide them there.
- Accounts that break the rules can be suspended.

Questions about anything Connect? **info@imotara.com**.
