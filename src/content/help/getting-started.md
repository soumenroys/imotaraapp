# Getting Started with Imotara

A friendly walkthrough of your first steps: what Imotara is, using it with or without an account, setting up your companion, and the basics of chatting.

## What is Imotara?

Imotara is a private, emotion-aware AI companion that helps you understand your feelings, track your mood over time, and grow emotionally — without ads, without data tracking, and without paywalls interrupting your conversations. It's available as a web app at www.imotara.com, plus iOS and Android apps. Imotara is a wellness companion for self-reflection and emotional awareness — it is **not** therapy and is not a replacement for professional mental health care. If you ever need clinical help, please reach out to a professional or the crisis helplines listed in the app (see our Safety & Crisis article).

## Using Imotara without an account

You don't need to sign up to start. Here's how:

1. Open the app — on web, go to **www.imotara.com**; on mobile, open the Imotara app.
2. Tap **Chat** and start typing. Text chat, mood check-ins, History, Trends, Grow, and reflections all work without signing in — your data is stored locally on your device.
3. Keep in mind what anonymous use *can't* do: cross-device sync, cloud backup, and unlimited voice aren't available. Read-aloud has a small daily cap (about 15 per day) and voice transcription about 20 per day when you're not signed in.
4. Cloud-generated features (Companion's Letter, Growth Arc, Year in Review) need an account and an internet connection.
5. If you reach the free daily limit of 20 cloud replies, Imotara quietly switches to on-device replies — the conversation is never blocked.

## Creating an account

Signing in is what turns on cross-device sync, cloud backup, and unlimited voice.

**On web:**
1. Go to **Settings** and find the **Remote history sync** card (or use the sign-in entry in the top bar).
2. Choose **Google** or **email magic link**. You'll be returned to the app once sign-in completes.
3. Confirm you're signed in: the **Remote history sync** card shows **Signed in as [your email]**.

**On mobile:**
1. Open **Settings** and expand the **Your plan** section.
2. Tap **Sign in to restore your plan** — mobile supports **Google** sign-in, and **Sign in with Apple** on iOS.
3. Once signed in, the **Your plan** card stops showing the sign-in prompt.

> Note: the email magic link is a **web-only** option. On mobile, please use Google or Apple sign-in. If you're on mobile and waiting for a sign-in email, that's the mix-up — use Google or Apple instead.

## Signing in on a second device

1. On the second device, sign in with the **same** account (same Google, email, or Apple identity).
2. Your history and profile pull down automatically from the cloud.
3. If you'd rather share history between devices without a full account, you can use the optional **Link Key** — see our Sync, Privacy & Account article.

## Setting up your companion

Your companion is yours to shape — a name, a vibe, a way of speaking that feels right to you.

**Where to find it:**
- **On web:** **Settings → Tone & Context Preferences** (it's optional, and stored locally).
- **On mobile:** **Settings → Your companion** (tap the section header to expand). You can also open it from Chat by swiping left-to-right if **Swipe right — Your Companion** is enabled.

**Steps:**
1. First, set your own details (they only shape wording and are never shared): your **Name (optional)**, **Age range**, **Gender**, and **Preferred language**.
2. Turn on the companion settings — on web, flip the **Expected companion tone** switch on.
3. Set a **Companion name (optional)** — anything that feels right.
4. Set the companion's **Age range** and **Gender**. Gender matters because it also chooses the read-aloud voice. Options: **Prefer not to say / Female / Male / Non-binary / Other**.
5. Pick a **Relationship vibe**: **Mentor, Elder, Friend, Coach, Sibling (younger/peer vibe), Junior buddy (younger vibe), Parent-like (tone only), Partner-like (tone only)**, or **Prefer not to specify**. This changes warmth and framing only — it's tone guidance, not identity simulation.
6. Pick a **Response style**: **Let Imotara decide, Comfort me — be present & warm, Help me reflect — ask gentle questions, Motivate me — be encouraging & energetic,** or **Give advice — practical next steps**. A one-line example of the chosen style appears under the picker, and you can always override it in conversation ("just listen today").
7. **On web only:** tap **🔊 Preview voice** under the companion Gender to hear how the voice sounds.
8. **On web**, press **Save** (top-right of the card). **On mobile**, changes save automatically and sync to your account when you're signed in. To start over, use **Reset** (web).

## Chat basics

### Sending a message
1. Open **Chat**.
2. Type in the message box at the bottom and press Enter or the send arrow.
3. **On web:** start a fresh conversation with the **New conversation** button at the top of the thread list. You can also **Rename** or **Delete thread** from the same list.

### Mood hint chips
1. In **Settings**, enable **Sentiment seed chips** ("Show quick-tap mood hint chips above the message input") — on web this is under **Chat behaviour**.
2. In Chat, tap a chip above the composer to give your message a mood hint.
3. First-time users see gentle starter chips like **Overwhelmed, Anxious, Low, Okay, Good, Just exploring** under "How are you feeling right now?" — tap one to begin.

### Quick panels (mobile)
1. **On mobile → Settings → Quick panels**, enable **Swipe right — Your Companion** and/or **Swipe left — Plan & Support**.
2. In Chat, swipe left-to-right to open companion settings, or right-to-left to open plan & support.

## Breathing exercises

Sometimes the kindest thing is to just breathe for a minute.

1. In the Chat composer, tap the **breathing** button — on web the label toggles **Open breathing exercise** / **Close breathing exercise**.
2. Pick a pattern:
   - **Box Breathing** — Inhale 4 / Hold 4 / Exhale 4 / Hold 4
   - **4-7-8 Calm** — Inhale 4 / Hold 7 / Exhale 8
   - **Simple Breath** — Inhale 4 / Exhale 6
3. **On mobile**, you can also pick a background sound: **Silent, Bowl, Rain, Ocean**.
4. Set your default pattern in **Settings → Grow & Wellbeing → Default breathing pattern** (web) or the matching **Default breathing pattern** picker (mobile).

The breathing screen also opens automatically if Imotara senses you're in real distress, alongside crisis helplines for your country.

## Finding your way around Settings (mobile)

1. Open **Settings** on mobile. At the top is a search box: **"Search settings or describe what you need..."**
2. Type a keyword ("voice", "breathing", "delete") or a whole sentence — Imotara will find the matching setting.
3. Tap a result to jump straight to that section.
