# Mood, History & Insights

How to log mood check-ins, browse and manage your History, read your Trends, write reflections in Grow, and enjoy Imotara's deeper features like Companion Letters, Growth Arc, Year in Review, and letters to your future self.

## Mood check-ins (Feel)

1. **On web:** open **Feel** in the top bar (www.imotara.com/feel). The header reads **"How are you feeling?"**
2. Tap one of the mood tiles: **Joyful, Calm, Sad, Anxious, Stressed, Angry, Lonely, Surprised**.
3. Optionally add a note in "What's on your mind right now…" and tap **Log check-in**.
4. A **🔥 streak** counter shows your consecutive days, and recent check-ins appear under **Recent**.
5. A quick check-in is also embedded inside the **Grow** page, so you can log a mood while reflecting.

## History — browse, search, and delete

Your History is a calm timeline of emotional moments from your conversations and check-ins.

1. Open **History** (the History tab, or the **View Emotion History** link in Settings).
2. **Browse:** each entry is a moment from your conversations — open one to see detail or jump back to Chat.
3. **Search — On web:** tap **Search**, then type in **"Search by emotion or message…"**. **On mobile:** tap the search toggle and type; you can also pick an emotion filter (default is **all**).
4. **Delete a single entry — On mobile:** swipe the row left to reveal the red **Delete** action and tap it. **On web:** manage entries from the History controls; bulk removal lives under **Settings → Local data controls**.

### How long history is kept (by plan)
- **Free:** the last **7 days**.
- **Plus:** the last **90 days**.
- **Pro:** **unlimited**.

A gentle banner offers an upgrade when your plan caps your visible history. (During the current launch offer, everything is unlocked for everyone — see the Plans & Payments article.)

## Trends — charts, streaks, and patterns

1. **On mobile**, open the **Trends** tab. You'll find:
   - A **streak card** ("[N] days in a row" — a spark appears at 3+ days, a flame at 7+).
   - A **reflection journal streak**.
   - A **weekly mood recap** and weekly emotion-frequency bars.
   - A **30-day mood line chart** and an emotion radar chart.
2. A small notification badge on the Trends tab clears when you open it. You can turn discovery tips on or off with **Settings → Feature tips**.
3. **On web**, the equivalent charts (emotion radar, 12-week mood heatmap) live on the **Grow** page. Emotion insights are a **Pro** feature, shown as a preview on lower plans.

## Grow — reflections and journaling

1. Open **Grow**.
2. Read **Today's prompt** and write in "Write what comes to mind — there's no wrong answer…", then tap **Save reflection** (⌘/Ctrl+Enter also saves).
3. Prompts personalise to your recent emotional arc. Tap **↻ Try another** for a different one, or **Use general instead** to swap the personalised prompt for a general one.
4. Grow also holds:
   - The **30-Day Challenge**.
   - The **Daily Journal** with **Today's prompt**.
   - **Themes I notice in your reflections** — patterns Imotara gently spots.
   - **Past reflections** — expand any entry to **Edit** or **Delete** it.
5. A mini weekly arc (your last 7 days of emotions, with "mostly [emotion]") appears at the top of the Grow page.

## Companion's Letter

Every so often, your companion writes you a letter reflecting on your journey together.

1. Letters are generated automatically on a schedule you choose: **Settings → Companion insights → Companion letter — every [7/14/30/60] days** (a **Pro** feature; on web, open the **Advanced** section).
2. Read past letters in **Settings → Companion insights → 💌 Letters from Imotara**. Expand a letter, then use **🔊 Listen** (becomes **⏹ Stop**), **React** with an emoji, or **Write reply / Edit reply** → **Save reply**.
3. **On mobile**, letters also surface as a **Companion Letter card** with a **Listen** button.
4. Letters need an account and an internet connection to generate.

## Growth Arc (Emotional Arc)

1. This is a narrative summary of your emotional journey, written on a schedule: **Settings → Companion insights → Emotional arc — every [7/14/30/60] days** (a **Pro** feature).
2. It reads like a gentle story of where you've been and how you've grown — not a report card.

## Year in Review

1. Open **History**. The **Year in Review** card ("Your [year] emotional journey") appears automatically once you have enough history.
2. Tap **Read →** to expand the narrative. Use **Copy** to copy it, or **Collapse** to close.

## Future Letters (write to your future self)

1. Open **Grow** and find **Letter to future self**.
2. Tap **+ Write**, type your note ("Dear future me…"), choose an unlock delay — **7 days / 30 days / 90 days / 180 days / 1 year** — and save. You'll see "Letter sealed — unlocks in [N] days ✓".
3. The letter stays locked until its unlock date, then appears in the unlocked list. You can delete a letter from the same section.

## Unsent Letters (write to someone you can't send to)

Sometimes there are words we never got to say. The Unsent Letter is a private, cathartic space for them.

1. **On mobile:** open the **Unsent Letter** flow (it appears as a gentle chat hint if **Unsent Letter hint** is enabled). Answer **"Who is this letter to?"**, add the relationship or context (e.g. "They passed away last year. We never got to say goodbye."), then continue.
2. Write your letter in Chat. Imotara replies in that person's voice — softly, and only for as long as you want. **On web**, the chat shows "Writing to [name] — Imotara will respond in their voice"; tap the ✕ to leave that mode any time.

## Companion memory — view, edit, delete

Imotara remembers meaningful things you share (your name, work, people who matter) so replies feel personal. You're always in control of what it keeps.

1. Go to **Settings → Companion memory** (on web, open the **Advanced** section).
2. **View:** each remembered fact shows its value and a confidence level. If it's empty you'll see "No memories yet — Imotara will pick up facts as you chat."
3. **Refresh** the list with the **Refresh** button.
4. **Delete one fact:** tap **Forget** on that row.
5. **Control what's captured:** turn **Companion memory auto-capture** on or off (**Settings → Chat behaviour** on web), and set the **Memory cap** (the maximum number of facts kept) under **Companion memory**.

Nothing you delete from memory is used again, and memories are never shared or shown to anyone else.
