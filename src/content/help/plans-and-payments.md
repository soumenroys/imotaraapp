# Plans & Payments

Imotara's plans and pricing, how to upgrade on web, Android, and iOS, token packs, cancelling, donations, and what to do if a payment doesn't show up.

## Buying a plan on the web (Razorpay)

1. Go to **imotara.com/upgrade** (or **Settings → Your plan → View plans & upgrade →**).
2. Pick **Plus** or **Pro** and press **Subscribe**.
3. Complete payment in the **Razorpay** checkout window (cards, UPI, and more).
4. Your plan activates right after the payment verifies. It can take a few seconds — if it doesn't show immediately, press **Refresh** on the **Your plan** card.

## Buying a plan on Android

1. **Settings → Your plan → Upgrade your plan** opens the upgrade sheet. You'll be asked to sign in first so the purchase is linked to your account (and can be restored later).
2. Pick a plan and tap **Subscribe** — payment goes through Google Play billing.
3. If it doesn't activate right away, give it a few seconds, then tap **Restore previous plan** (or **Settings → Your plan → Already purchased? Tap to check your plan**).

## Buying a plan on iOS

1. Open the upgrade sheet and tap **Subscribe** — payment goes through Apple's in-app purchase.
2. If activation is pending, or you already bought the plan on your Apple account (say, on another device), tap **Restore previous purchases** to link it to your profile.

## Restore purchases

- **iOS:** upgrade sheet → **Restore previous purchases**. This re-checks your active subscriptions with Apple and re-applies them to your account.
- **Android:** upgrade sheet → **Restore previous plan**; or **Settings → Your plan → Already purchased? Tap to check your plan**.
- Purchases are tied to your **Imotara account**, not the device — so on a new phone, just sign in with the same account and restore.

## Token packs (message credits)

Token packs are a friendly one-time top-up for free users: **one credit = one extra cloud reply beyond your daily 20**. Credits **never expire** and don't change your plan.

| Pack | Price | Credits |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | ₹49 | 100 |
| Regular | ₹99 | 250 |
| Value | ₹199 | 600 |
| Best value | ₹499 | 1,800 |

- **On web:** buy them on **imotara.com/upgrade** under top-ups (paid via Razorpay).
- **On mobile:** the upgrade sheet has a **Top up with message credits** section (purchased through the app store).

Credits are used automatically: once you pass 20 cloud replies in a day, one credit is spent per extra reply. When credits run out, Imotara switches to on-device replies as usual.

## Cancelling a subscription

How you cancel depends on where you subscribed:

- **Web (Razorpay):** **Settings → Your plan → Cancel subscription → Confirm cancel**. You keep full access until the end of the billing period you already paid for, then your account returns to Free.
- **iOS:** **Settings → Your plan → Manage subscription → Manage in App Store →**, then cancel in your Apple subscription settings. Apple subscriptions can **only** be cancelled through Apple — Imotara cannot cancel them for you.
- **Android:** **Settings → Your plan → Manage subscription → Manage in Google Play →**, then cancel in your Play Store subscription settings. Same story — Google subscriptions can only be cancelled in Google Play.

In every case, you keep access until the end of the period you paid for. Nothing is deleted when a plan lapses — your account simply returns to Free limits.

## Donations

Imotara exists so emotional support isn't gated behind affordability. If you'd like to support that mission:

1. **On web:** **Settings → Support Imotara (Donate)** — tap a preset amount and complete the Razorpay checkout. Your receipt appears under **Your Donations** (press **Refresh** to reload). The **imotara.com/donate** page offers the same presets.
2. **On iOS:** tips go through Apple's tip jar — tap a tip amount ("Processed securely by Apple").

Donations are separate from subscriptions — they don't change your plan, they just keep the lights warm. Thank you.

## "I paid but nothing happened"

Don't worry — payments are verified independently on our side, so a hiccup in your browser or app right after paying almost always sorts itself out. Try these in order:

1. **Wait a few seconds and refresh.** Activation usually lands within moments. On web, press **Refresh** on the **Your plan** card; on mobile, close and reopen the upgrade sheet.
2. **On mobile, use Restore.** iOS: **Restore previous purchases**. Android: **Restore previous plan**. This re-checks your purchase and applies it.
3. **UPI payments** can take an extra minute to confirm — give it a moment before retrying, so you don't pay twice.
4. **Still nothing after a few minutes?** Email **info@imotara.com** with your payment reference (the payment ID from Razorpay, or your Apple/Google order number) and which plan or pack you bought. We'll verify it and set your account right — and if you were ever charged twice for the same thing, we'll make sure one is refunded on the same payment method you used.

### Refunds, briefly
- **Web (Razorpay):** contact us at **info@imotara.com** with your payment reference.
- **iOS:** Apple handles its own refunds — use Apple's "Report a Problem" flow.
- **Android:** request through Google Play, or write to us and we'll help.
