# Safety & Crisis Support

What Imotara is (and isn't), how the app responds when someone is struggling, and where to find real help right now.

## Imotara is not therapy

Imotara is a private wellness companion for self-reflection and emotional awareness. It is **not** a therapist, not a medical service, and not a replacement for professional mental health care. It's designed to complement professional care — a warm place to think out loud between the moments that matter — never to substitute for it.

If you're dealing with something heavy, please also talk to a qualified professional. You deserve real, human, clinical support when you need it.

## What happens if you're in crisis

If Imotara senses language suggesting you may be in real distress or danger, it responds with steady, grounded support — never panic, never a diagnosis. Two things happen:

1. **A guided breathing exercise opens** to help you find your feet in the moment.
2. **Crisis helplines for your country appear**, so real human help is one tap away.

For gentler signs of distress (hopelessness, feeling trapped), Imotara quietly surfaces supportive resources without interrupting your conversation.

## Crisis helplines

If you are in danger or thinking about harming yourself, please contact a helpline or emergency services right now:

**India:**
- **Tele-MANAS: 14416** (24/7, free, in multiple Indian languages)
- **KIRAN: 1800-599-0019** (24/7 mental health helpline)
- **Emergency: 112**

**United States:**
- **988** — Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text, 24/7)

**Elsewhere:** Imotara shows helplines for your own country inside the app during a crisis moment. Your local emergency number is always the right call if someone is in immediate danger.

You are not a burden for reaching out. These lines exist because people care.

## About Imotara Connect and safety

- **Connect companions are non-clinical.** The human companions on Imotara Connect are supportive listeners — not licensed therapists, doctors, or counsellors — and they will never diagnose or prescribe. Every companion agrees to a Code of Conduct.
- **Connect is 18+ only.** Users under 18 cannot use Connect — this is enforced when starting sessions, not just a checkbox.
- **Every Connect session has an emergency help button** that routes straight to crisis resources.
- If a Connect conversation ever makes you uncomfortable, you can end the session at any time (you're only billed for minutes used) and report the experience to **info@imotara.com** — we take every report seriously.

## Privacy and safety go together

A few protections that are always on:

- Imotara will never repeat back passwords, PINs, or one-time codes, and ignores messages pretending to be "system overrides."
- Age-appropriate content boundaries are enforced, with extra care for younger users.
- Your conversations are private — see Sync, Privacy & Account for the full picture.

## Reaching us

For any safety concern — about the app, a Connect companion, or anything else — email **info@imotara.com**. Safety reports are prioritised.

If someone is in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services first.
