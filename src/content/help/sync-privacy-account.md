# Sync, Privacy & Your Account

How cross-device sync works, what Imotara stores (and pointedly does not collect), your privacy controls, exporting your data, and deleting your account.

## Our privacy promise, in short

- By default, your conversations live **locally on your own device**.
- Cloud sync and cloud analysis only happen **with your consent** — you choose the mode.
- **No ads. No third-party tracking. No analytics on your emotional content. Your data is never sold.** There are no public profiles and no social feed.
- Cloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and only you can access your own records.
- You can export or delete your data at any time, on **every plan** — including Free.

## Cross-device sync

Signing in is what turns on cloud backup and sync.

### Turn on cloud sync / back up now
1. Sign in first (see Getting Started).
2. **On web:** **Settings → Remote history sync → Back up now** pushes your local history to the cloud and pulls records from your other devices. A status line appears below the button.
3. **On mobile:** sync runs automatically when you're signed in — new items are pushed shortly after they're created, and again whenever the app returns to the foreground. Make sure **Analysis Mode** is set to **Auto** or **Online** (see below) — **Local** mode keeps everything on-device and intentionally does not sync.

### Force a sync
1. **On web:** press **Back up now**.
2. **On mobile:** bring the app to the foreground, or leave the chat open briefly — a sync runs on its own.

### Link devices with a Link Key (optional)
The Link Key is a private phrase that lets devices share the same chat history — handy if you want to link devices without relying only on account sign-in.

1. **On web:** **Settings → Sync with another device (optional)**. Type a private phrase (no spaces, roughly 8–20 characters) into "Paste a Link Key…" and press **Save** (use **Copy** to share it with your other device, **Clear** to remove it).
2. **On mobile:** **Settings → Privacy & safety → Link this device (optional)**. Enter the **same** Link Key and tap **Save** (**Clear** to remove).
3. Devices with the same Link Key share the same chat history. **Treat the key like a password** — anyone who has it can see that shared history.

### Sync status indicators
1. **On mobile:** turn on **Settings → Show sync status** ("Show reply source on messages") to see a small badge on each message telling you whether the reply came from the cloud or from on-device mode.
2. **On web:** the Chat top bar shows a sync chip, and the **History** legend explains the dots: **green ●** = synced entries, **amber ●** = entries with conflicts you can review in the Conflicts panel.

## What's stored where

- **Not signed in:** everything stays on your device. Nothing is backed up — if you uninstall the app or clear your browser data, that data is gone. (Sign in if you want a backup.)
- **Signed in, sync on:** your history and profile are backed up to Imotara's secure cloud so they follow you across devices. Only your account can access your records.
- **Your companion and tone preferences** are stored locally and only shape how Imotara talks to you — they aren't shared.

## Analysis modes (Auto / Online / Local)

You choose how much goes to the cloud:

1. **On mobile:** **Settings → Privacy & safety → Analysis Mode** — choose:
   - **Auto** — tries the cloud, falls back to on-device when needed (recommended).
   - **Online** — always cloud, for the highest quality.
   - **Local** — device-only. Nothing is sent anywhere. (Note: Local mode also means no cloud sync and no cross-device history — that's the point.)
2. **On web:** the current mode is shown in **Settings → Emotion analysis mode**, and it's **changed from the Chat page** using the analysis toggle (Local only / Auto routing / Remote allowed). In **Auto**, on-device analysis runs first and the cloud is only used when the local result isn't confident.

## The free daily limit — and what happens when you hit it

Free accounts get **20 cloud AI replies per day** (the counter resets at midnight UTC). When you reach it, Imotara doesn't lock you out — it quietly switches to its on-device reply mode so the conversation continues. On-device replies are simpler than cloud replies, but you're never blocked. To keep cloud replies flowing, you can buy a token pack, upgrade, or simply wait for the daily reset — see Plans & Payments.

## Offline behavior

On mobile, Imotara keeps working with no internet: an on-device reply engine answers you in all 22 languages, and you can read previously loaded history. A few things do need a connection: voice input, read-aloud, and the cloud-generated features (Companion's Letter, Growth Arc, Year in Review). The web app is also installable and works offline for browsing.

## Export your data

1. **On web:** **Settings → Export data → Download JSON** downloads your local chat history. A fuller export lives on the **History** page via **Export JSON** (the complete emotion history export is a **Pro** feature).
2. **On mobile:** **Settings → Privacy & safety** offers **Export JSON**, **Export CSV**, and **Export Journal** — each opens your phone's share sheet.
3. A complete, machine-readable copy of all data held about your account is available to everyone on request — if you can't get what you need in-app, email **info@imotara.com**.

## Clear local data

1. **On web:** **Settings → Local data controls** → **Clear Chat conversations**, **Clear Emotion History**, or **Clear ALL local Imotara data**. You can also set **Auto-delete chat threads older than** a chosen age here.
2. **On mobile:** **Settings → Clear Local History** clears this device; **Clear Remote Data** deletes your cloud records too (requires sign-in).

## Delete your account

Deletion is available on every plan, including Free, and removes your cloud-stored data — conversations, memories, and settings.

1. **On web:** **Settings → Delete account**. When signed in, the button is **Delete my account** ("Permanently delete your Imotara account and all associated cloud data — conversations, memories, and settings. This cannot be undone."). When signed out, the same spot offers **Clear all local data** instead.
2. **On mobile:** sign in, then use the delete-account control in Settings; or use **Clear Remote Data** if you only want to remove cloud history while keeping the account.
3. Local data on your devices is cleared separately (see "Clear local data" above) or by uninstalling the app.
4. **This cannot be undone.** If you hit any trouble completing a deletion or data request in-app, email **info@imotara.com** and we'll take care of it.

## A note on guest data

If you use Imotara without signing in, your data exists only on that device. Reinstalling the app or clearing browser storage removes it permanently — there is no way for us to recover it, because we never had it. If your history matters to you, sign in so it's backed up.
